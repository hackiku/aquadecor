L 2 - Juwel 3D Aquarium Background

About Aquadecor "L" Models. Flexible and durable. If needed, we make them in several pieces (top supports on the aquarium are not an issue). Don’t need to be siliconed and can be installed in a full aquarium. Hollow at the back (all equipment can be placed behind the background). The "L" models are crafted to fit seamlessly into a variety of tank configurations, accommodating the unique needs of your aquatic environment. One of the standout features of our "L" Models is their flexibility. If necessary, we can create these backgrounds in several pieces to ensure a perfect fit, even around top supports or other obstacles within the aquarium. This modular design approach means you don’t have to worry about complex installations or modifications to your tank. Our "L" models do not require silicone for installation, making the setup process straightforward and hassle-free. You can easily install them in a fully stocked aquarium without disturbing your aquatic life. The backgrounds are hollow at the back, which provides ample space for placing equipment such as filters, heaters, and cables. This thoughtful design feature ensures that all your essential equipment remains neatly tucked away, preserving the visual appeal of your tank while maintaining easy access for maintenance. The durability of our "L" models ensures they stand up to the rigors of aquarium life, resisting wear and tear while providing a stunning visual backdrop. Whether you’re upgrading an existing setup or designing a new one, our "L" Models offer a practical and stylish solution that enhances both the functionality and appearance of your aquarium.
